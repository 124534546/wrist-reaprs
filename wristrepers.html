Toproll Technique

The toproll is a technique focused on attacking your opponent’s fingers and hand. You cup your wrist, rise your knuckles, and pronate hard to peel back their grip. Toprollers focus on back pressure and hand control. It’s great for tall armwrestlers or those with long levers.

Hook Technique

The hook is an inside move, focusing on dragging the opponent's arm and wrist inward toward your body. It uses wrist flexion, elbow drag, and intense side pressure. Perfect for those with strong biceps and a powerful forearm lock. In a hook battle, arm strength and endurance matter most.

Press Technique

The press is a brute force move used especially off the start. You press your shoulder behind your hand and drive downward over your opponent's hand. Pressing requires strong triceps and chest strength. It’s a risky but fast-ending move when done right.
